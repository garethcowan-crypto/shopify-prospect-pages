Discovery Findings
What We Heard
Key challenges and needs from our conversation with Aaron.
🕐

After-Hours Ordering Gap

Your grocery customers can't place orders when Potato King is closed. Add-on orders (5 more cases of lettuce) have to wait until morning, or your sales team comes in on Saturdays.

📅

Saturday Staffing Cost

Sales reps come in half-days on Saturdays to take phone orders. A self-service portal eliminates that cost and gives your team their weekends back.

⚠️

Previous Portal Failed (Consentio)

Your last B2B portal (Consentio, Spain-based) was dismantled 2 months ago due to excessive downtime and 24-hour support response times. Customers got frustrated.

📞

Phone-Heavy Ordering Process

The vast majority of orders come by phone with lots of back-and-forth ("how do the cherries look today?"). You're not trying to replace that — you want to supplement it for quick add-ons.

💲

Complex Pricing Structure

Multiple price points, customer groups, date-based specials, customer-specific pricing. "Kind of all over the place." Needs to sync from Dynamics 365.

🧑‍💻

One-Person IT Team

Aaron manages IT alongside finance and operations. The platform needs to be self-manageable without requiring developer support day-to-day.
