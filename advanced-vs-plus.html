Area Advanced (Self-Serve ToS) Plus (Negotiated MSA)
Indemnification One-way. Customer indemnifies Shopify only. Shopify carries no indemnification obligations to the merchant. Mutual. Shopify indemnifies the merchant against IP infringement claims and DPA breaches. Risk shared equally.
Liability Cap Not specified. No contractual ceiling on Shopify's liability — effectively uncapped under standard boilerplate. Capped at 12 months of Platform Fees paid. Merchant has a defined, predictable ceiling on recoverable damages.
Confidentiality None. No confidentiality provisions. Business data shared with Shopify carries no contractual protection. Full mutual obligations. Both parties are bound by confidentiality, with injunctive relief available as a remedy for breach.
IP License to Shopify Broad, open-ended grant. Merchant grants Shopify a wide IP license with no defined end date or scope restriction. Scoped and time-limited. License is restricted to the duration of the Term and solely to provide the Services.
Bottom line: Advanced is a click-through, self-serve agreement — protections are one-sided in Shopify's favour. Plus is a negotiated commercial contract with mutual indemnification, a defined liability cap, confidentiality obligations, and joint control over amendments. For any merchant with legal, procurement, or compliance involvement in the purchase decision, the Plus MSA is a materially different document from what they'd be signing on Advanced.

Together they give merchants complete control over the highest-intent moment in the buyer journey — something Advanced merchants cannot access.

UI Extensions — What You Can Build
🛒
Pre-Payment Upsells Plus
Inject product upsell offers directly into the checkout — before the buyer completes payment. One-click add to order, no re-entering payment details. Target by cart contents, order value, customer segment, or product type. Common placements: after cart summary, on the payment page, or between shipping and payment steps.
Business Impact: Checkout upsells convert at 3–5x the rate of post-purchase popups because your buyer already has their payment details entered and intent is at its peak. It's the highest-converting upsell placement available — and it's only accessible on Plus.

Cross-Sell Recommendations Plus
Display complementary product recommendations at checkout — accessories, consumables, warranties, or bundles. Powered by your own logic or third-party recommendation engines. Shown at the cart or payment step when buyer intent is highest.
Business Impact: Cross-sells at checkout increase your average order value without any additional acquisition spend. The same buyer spends more in the same session — pure margin improvement on traffic you've already paid for.
🎁
Post-Purchase Upsell Pages Plus
Add a post-purchase page between "Place Order" and the order confirmation — a dedicated upsell moment after payment is captured but before the thank you page. Buyers can add to their order without re-entering payment details. The most frictionless upsell point in ecommerce.
Business Impact: Post-purchase upsells carry zero risk to your primary conversion — the order is already placed. Every buyer who accepts is incremental revenue with no impact on your checkout completion rate.
📝
Custom Fields & Order Notes Plus
Add custom input fields to checkout — gift messages, delivery instructions, engraving text, PO numbers, installation dates, or any business-specific data. Fields are saved to order metafields and flow through to your OMS, ERP, or fulfilment system automatically.
Business Impact: Capturing PO numbers, delivery instructions, and custom fields at checkout eliminates manual follow-up and reduces fulfilment errors — saving your operations team time on every B2B order. Custom Consent & Compliance UI Plus
Inject custom consent checkboxes, age verification prompts, regulatory disclosures, and terms acceptance directly into checkout. Required for age-restricted products, regulated industries, subscription terms, or markets with specific consent requirements (GDPR, CCPA).
Business Impact: Capturing consent natively within checkout gives you a cleaner, more auditable record than bolted-on popups or pre-checkout consent pages. It's better for your buyers' experience and stronger from a compliance standpoint.
🏷️
Dynamic Banners & Promotions Plus
Display contextual banners at checkout — free shipping thresholds, loyalty points earned on this order, limited-time discount countdowns, or bundle incentives. Banners update dynamically based on cart contents and order value.
Business Impact: Buyers sometimes abandon checkout when they second-guess the value of their purchase. Dynamic incentive banners at the payment step — free shipping thresholds, loyalty point previews, limited-time offers — reduce that hesitation and can measurably lift your checkout completion rate.

Loyalty & Rewards Integration Plus
Surface loyalty point balances, reward redemption, and tier status directly inside checkout — no redirect, no external widget. Buyers see their points, choose how to apply them, and complete the order in one flow. Integrates with Smile, LoyaltyLion, Yotpo, and other major loyalty platforms.

B2B Approval & Validation UI Plus
For B2B checkout flows, inject approval workflows, purchase order entry, credit limit validation, and buyer authorisation directly into checkout. Display company-specific messaging, payment terms, and account information — all scoped to the logged-in company account.
Business Impact: Your enterprise B2B buyers have procurement processes that don't fit a standard consumer checkout. With Plus, you can build the checkout around their requirements — approval workflows, PO capture, account-specific payment terms — rather than asking them to adapt to yours.

Override Shopify's shipping rate calculation with fully custom delivery logic — hide, rename, reorder, or inject shipping methods based on cart contents, customer tags, delivery address, order value, or B2B company.
