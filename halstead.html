Evaluation Summary
What We've Learned So Far
Through our discovery calls, reverse demo, and technical review with Jason and the team, here's what we understand about your business and what's driving this evaluation.
💸

$30K Patch Quote Was the Breaking Point

Znode quoted $30K for a minor version patch (v9.7 → v9.9). You're currently paying ~$3K/mo for the platform. The cost-to-value equation no longer makes sense.

🧑‍💻

One-Person IT Team

Jason maintains the entire self-hosted Znode stack alone — ASP.NET MVC on SQL Server, custom integrations for tax (ZipTax), CMS (Butter), CDN (Cloudflare), and Braintree. Every change is a bottleneck.

💳

Card Vaulting Without Pre-Auth

Your payment flow is non-standard: Braintree vaults the card at checkout, then Sage X3 references the token later during fulfillment to charge. Shopify B2B's native card vaulting supports this workflow.

🔗

Sage X3 Integration

Orders and customers push from website → ERP. Products, pricing, and inventory push from ERP → website. Two-way sync with Sage X3 is critical to your operations.

📊

Wholesale + Retail Pricing Complexity

Wholesale pricing is a discount off base retail, with quantity-based tiered pricing at both levels. Shopify B2B handles this with native price lists and quantity rules.

🔒

Purchase Approval Workflows

Anyone can create a profile, but wholesale customers go through an application process. Shopify B2B company accounts with approval flows map directly to this requirement.

Cost Analysis
Your Savings on Shopify Plus
Based on our review of your merchant statements and current platform costs, here's the financial picture.

Current Platform Costs (Znode)

  • ~$3,000/month platform licensing
  • $30K quoted for a single minor patch
  • Self-hosted infrastructure (servers, SQL Server, SSL, PCI)
  • Custom integrations maintained by Jason

Shopify Plus

  • $2,300/month flat — all hosting, security, PCI, and updates included
  • No patching costs — Shopify updates automatically
  • Shopify Payments: 2.25% + 30¢ (online), 2.25% (in-person)
  • 8,000+ apps for integrations — no custom code required

Estimated Annual Savings

  • ~$47,000+/yr in payment processing savings
  • ~$21,000+/yr in platform cost savings
  • Eliminates $30K+ patch surprises
  • Frees Jason from infrastructure maintenance
