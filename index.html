Current Challenges

What We Heard from Your Team

From our discovery with Tim, Andrew, and Emily — these are the problems holding Profillment back.

🛑
No Purchase Approval Workflows
Hotel GMs place orders that need regional supervisor approval. Orders over $3,000 need a third-tier approver. Your current homegrown site can't do this — and it's your #1 requirement.
🏨
No Custom Catalogs per Property
Each hotel property needs to see only the items with the correct finishes and designs for their location. Today this is manual or non-existent — leading to wrong orders and returns.
📦
Split Fulfillment Is Complex
Orders split between your warehouse and vendor drop-ship. Acumatica handles routing, but your homegrown site doesn't support split fulfillment natively.
💰
Can't Process Payments Online
Freight costs are estimated, not exact — so you don't process payments on the website at all. Invoices are sent separately after shipping. This creates friction and manual work.
🔄
Operational Silos
Ecommerce, inventory, shipping, and ERP are disconnected systems. Your small team spends too much time bridging gaps manually instead of growing the business.
📉
Homegrown Site Can't Scale
Your current platform was built for where you were, not where you're going. Scaling the reordering business and deploying self-service requires a modern foundation.

Your Challenges, Mapped to Solutions

Every requirement from your discovery — addressed.

YOUR CHALLENGE
Tiered Purchase Approval Workflows
Hotel GMs need supervisor approval. Orders >$3K need third-tier approval. Email notifications to reviewers who can approve, deny, or edit.
SHOPIFY PLUS SOLUTION
Approovly + Shopify B2B
Native B2B company accounts combined with the Approovly app ($19–$59/mo) enables multi-tier approval workflows with email notifications, approve/deny from admin, and custom order tagging. Configurable per company.
YOUR CHALLENGE
Custom Catalogs per Hotel Property
Each property should only see items with correct finishes, designs, and brands for their specific location.
SHOPIFY PLUS SOLUTION
B2B Catalogs — Up to 10,000
Publish specific products and pricing per company location. Each hotel property gets their own catalog with only the products they should see. Managed centrally, experienced locally.
YOUR CHALLENGE
Drop-Ship + Warehouse Split Fulfillment
Orders split between your warehouse and vendor drop-ship items. Acumatica handles routing.
SHOPIFY PLUS SOLUTION
Acumatica Integration + Multi-Location
Acumatica has a native Shopify connector that supports split fulfillment routing. Orders flow to Acumatica, which routes to warehouse or vendor based on your existing logic. Multi-location inventory is native.
YOUR CHALLENGE
Freight Cost Estimation — Can't Charge Online
Freight is estimated, not exact. You invoice separately after shipping. No online payment processing.
SHOPIFY PLUS SOLUTION
Net Terms + Free Freight Threshold
Native payment terms (Net 30/60/90) let hotels order on account without credit cards. Offer free freight above a $2K minimum to simplify pricing. Invoicing handled through Acumatica integration.
