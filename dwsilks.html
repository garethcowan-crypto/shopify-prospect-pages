Conversation Summary
Key topics from our introductory call with Samantha — covering migration, self-service editing, wholesale, integrations, and more.
🔄

Migration from WordPress

Shopify handles WordPress/WooCommerce migrations regularly. Your site would be built on Shopify in the background while your current site stays live. When ready, we flip the DNS — zero downtime, no interruption to operations.

✨

Click, Not Code

Unlike WordPress where visual changes require a developer (like widening content to full landscape), Shopify's theme editor lets you make design changes yourself — no coding background needed. This eliminates the dependency on your agency for routine updates.

🏪

Wholesale B2B Portal

Shopify Plus natively supports gated wholesale portals — dealers apply, get approved, log in, and see your trade-only pricing. General consumers can't access products or pricing. Exactly how D&W Silks operates today, but built into the platform.

🔗

Sage ERP Integration

Shopify connects to Sage (all major versions) with pre-built connectors. Inventory, pricing, and item names sync between Sage and your storefront. Orders placed on Shopify flow back into Sage automatically.

👥

Repzio + Website

Today, your Repzio sales rep tool doesn't communicate with the website. On Shopify Plus, sales reps can place orders on behalf of customers through draft orders — and all orders (web + rep) flow through one system.

💳

Payment Processing

You can keep your current payment processor or switch to Shopify Payments for potentially better rates. There's a small additional fee (0.2%) if using a third-party processor on Plus. We can run a rate comparison to see which saves you more.

Variable Platform Fee (VPF) — When Does the Price Go Above $2,300/mo?
The VPF only applies when monthly GMV exceeds a threshold. With Shopify Payments, that threshold is ~$15.3M/year.
The Formula

Monthly cost = GREATER OF $2,300 OR (Monthly GMV × 0.18%)

VPF rate with Shopify Payments: 0.18%

Threshold: $2,300 ÷ 0.0018 = $1,277,778/mo

= ~$15.3 million per year

If you're transacting less than ~$15.3M/year through Shopify, your platform fee is a flat $2,300/month with zero variable charges.
