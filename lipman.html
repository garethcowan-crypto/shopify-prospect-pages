Discovery Findings
What We Heard
Key challenges surfaced during our conversation with Buck about Lipman Brothers' current digital ordering and operations.
🔒

Provi Controls the Relationship

Provi is a marketplace, not your channel. They control ad placement, search ranking, and brand visibility. Competitors are on the same platform. You lose the customer relationship.

🔌

Zero ERP Integration

Provi sends orders as emails. No sync with Dynamics GP or Pocket Advantage. Every order gets manually re-entered.

💲

Owner Blocks Tiered Pricing

Only frontline (highest) pricing is shown on Provi because the owner doesn't want competitors seeing discounted rates. This kills ecommerce utility.

📟

Outdated Order Entry

Pocket Advantage is from the 90s. No integration with Provi or the ERP. Sales reps are hog-tied by a system that can't keep up.

📱

Digital Experience Gap

Gen Z buyers want self-service. Bar and restaurant owners want to reorder at 2am after close. Competition is beating you on digital.

📉

Low Digital Adoption

Only 390 of 7,000 customers (5%) use Provi. Only 1,600 of 10,000 SKUs listed. ~$2.5M estimated GMV through the platform.

Mapping your requirements to native Shopify Plus B2B capabilities, app ecosystem solutions, and areas requiring custom work.

Gated pricing / no public access Native Login required to see any pricing. Products visible or hidden, your choice.

Regional product gating Native Control which SKUs are available by county or distribution agreement.

Language auto-detection Native Browser detects Spanish, redirects to Spanish version automatically.
Open API for PowerBI Native GraphQL + REST. Pull any data into PowerBI dashboards.
