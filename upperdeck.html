Compliance & Verification

KYC / KYB Documentation

Shopify has identity and business verification built into the platform as part of Shopify Payments onboarding. Here’s what you need to know.

🪪
Identity Verification (KYC)
The store owner completes identity verification with a government-issued photo ID and a liveness check (selfie). Only the store owner is required to verify in most cases.

Identity Verification Guide →
🏢
Business Verification (KYB)
Submit business details — business type, EIN, address, and banking information. Shopify verifies the entity and activates payment processing.

Business Setup Requirements →
📸
Proof of Liveness
A selfie and photo of your physical ID (not a scan). All four corners visible, dark background, no glare. Both front and back if the document has two sides.

Liveness Check Details →
⚙️
Payments Account Setup
Step-by-step walkthrough of the full Shopify Payments setup — from personal info to business verification to activation.

Full Setup Walkthrough →
Enterprise Timeline
For enterprise merchants like Upper Deck, verification typically takes 5–7 business days due to the complexity of the business structure (multi-member LLC, C-Corp, international stakeholders). Two-step authentication must be enabled on the store before payouts can be activated.
