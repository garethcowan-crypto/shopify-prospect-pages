Licensing & Costs
Shopify Plus Pricing — Straightforward
One flat monthly fee. Payment processing on every transaction. No hidden charges, no hosting fees, no patching costs, no PCI compliance fees.
💳

Platform Fee

  • $2,300/month — flat subscription
  • Includes hosting, security, SSL, PCI compliance
  • Automatic platform updates at no cost
  • Up to 9 expansion stores included (e.g., SecureIt Garage Systems)
  • 99.99% uptime SLA
🔒

Payment Processing (Shopify Payments)

  • 2.25% + 30¢ — standard consumer card (online)
  • 3.25% + 30¢ — international cards
  • 2.95% + 30¢ — premium / commercial & Amex
  • 2.25% — in-person (POS tap/chip/swipe)
  • $15 — chargeback fee (refunded if you win)
📦

What You Don't Pay

  • No hosting fees — included
  • No SSL certificate fees — included
  • No PCI compliance costs — included
  • No security patching costs — automatic
  • No separate payment gateway fee
  • No $30K surprise patch quotes

What's Included
Everything You Get with Shopify Plus
No plugins to manage, no hosting to worry about, no developers needed for day-to-day operations.
🏪

Up to 9 Expansion Stores

Run SecureIt Gun Storage and SecureIt Garage Systems as separate storefronts under one account and one contract. Each gets its own domain, theme, catalog, and checkout.

📱

World-Class Mobile & Checkout

Shopify's checkout is proven to convert better than any other platform. Shop Pay delivers 50% higher conversion with one-tap mobile purchasing — critical when 66% of your traffic is mobile.

🔌

8,000+ App Ecosystem

Fishbowl, QuickBooks, Affirm, Klaviyo, shipping carriers — if you need it, there's an integration. No custom code required for most connections.

🎨

Built for Non-Technical Teams

Drag-and-drop theme editor, no developer needed for content updates. Pages look great on desktop and mobile simultaneously — no more image forcing or cut-off products.

Enterprise Security & Uptime

99.99% uptime SLA. PCI DSS Level 1 compliance included. Automatic security patches. DDoS protection via Cloudflare. Zero maintenance burden on your team.

Shopify Payments Built In

No separate payment gateway to manage. No outdated Stripe or PayPal plugins. Best-in-class rates at 2.25% + 30¢. Apple Pay, Google Pay, Shop Pay — all native.
