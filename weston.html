Challenges Driving This Evaluation
These are the key pain points Rick and Kara shared during our discovery call.
🔄

Epicor Eagle End of Life

Your all-in-one ERP + POS system of 15 years is reaching end of life. You're evaluating replacements for ERP, POS, ecommerce, and CRM simultaneously — a full tech stack overhaul.

🚫

No Online Transactions

You currently cannot process payments online. No buy-online-pickup-in-store, no online ordering, no self-service for customers. Revenue is being left on the table.

📧

Manual Wholesale Ordering

Wholesale customers order via email, phone, and text — with lots of back-and-forth. Landscapers are trying to place orders at 10:30 PM with no self-service option available.

📱

No Mobile POS

Sales reps can't check out customers in the yard. All transactions require going back to the office. No line busting, no mobile inventory receiving.

🧩

Fragmented Evaluation

ERP, POS, ecommerce, and CRM are being evaluated separately with different vendors. Risk of ending up with a cobbled-together stack that doesn't talk to itself.

💳

No Wholesale Portal

No gated B2B portal exists today. Wholesale customers can't log in, see their pricing, view order history, or reorder. 60% of your revenue runs through manual processes.
